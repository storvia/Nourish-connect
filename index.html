About The Platform

FoodLift ensures that donations are tracked, families are verified, and food vendors are accountable. Every donation is transparent and traceable through our platform.

How It Works

1. Donors Contribute

Individuals and organizations donate funds through the platform.

2. Families Verified

Beneficiaries are verified by administrators before receiving support.

3. Vouchers Issued

Approved families receive food vouchers tied to registered vendors.

4. Vendors Redeem

Vendors redeem vouchers and receive credited balances transparently.
